Welcome to Image Recommender

Greetings! I am Çağrı Çaycı, and I am thrilled to present my graduation project an image recommendation system hosted on a user-friendly website. The core objective of this system is to identify and recommend images that are most likely to resonate with users within a given dataset. Leveraging the capabilities of the Google Vision API, the system labels randomly generated images, establishing a robust foundation for accurate recommendations. The website serves as an interactive showcase, allowing users to witness the system's process in action. The working principle involves the generation of images, their meticulous labeling using Google Vision API, and subsequent scoring through an efficient algorithm. The culmination is the presentation of a single image with the highest score—a meticulously curated recommendation tailored to the user's preferences.
